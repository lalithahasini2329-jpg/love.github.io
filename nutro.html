Daily Nutrition Guide

Your path to a healthier lifestyle starts here

Recommended Daily Intake

Calories

2000-2500

Based on average adult needs

Protein

50-60g

Essential for muscle growth

Carbohydrates

225-325g

Primary energy source

Healthy Fats

44-77g

Important for brain health

Key Nutrients to Focus On

  • Fiber: 25-30g daily for digestive health
  • Vitamin D: 600-800 IU for bone health
  • Iron: 18mg for energy and blood health
  • Calcium: 1000mg for strong bones and teeth

Healthy Eating Tips

  1. Eat a rainbow of fruits and vegetables daily
  2. Choose whole grains over refined grains
  3. Include lean proteins in every meal
  4. Stay hydrated with 8 glasses of water daily
  5. Limit processed foods and added sugars
